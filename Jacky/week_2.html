# Updated Personas


Cassidy Doe

Birth: 3rd June 1990
Occupation: Head of HR
Status: Married, Mother of 2


Everyday Cassidy has to get up and go to work very early in the morning because she works far away from her workplace and also has to deal with traffic. Not to mention she also has to take care of her kids along side her husband that also has to get up early for work. This doesn’t give Cassidy any time for herself as she has to wake up early and come home late and still has to cook for her family.


She wishes that there was a better way to get to her workplace that doesn’t take as long and doesn’t have to be stuck in traffic so that should could get some time with her family and herself.

Problem Statement:

The problem we are trying to solve is the time wasted during commutes/waiting when traveling to destinations.


Proposition:

To use teleportation to reduce time taken to travel to places.
